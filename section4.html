Trend Analysis

STC(Series Test of Cluster)can be used to find the trend characteristics of gene expression，and concentrate on the genes with the same change characteristics in a changing trend.

Methods:

From the results of THCA genes expression obtained from TCGA database，the median gene expression was grouped according to the sample subtypesEach subtype was used as a time point, from left to right are Control、Classical、Follicular and TallCell (not shown in the figure），the specific expression genes different from other subtypes were selected from subdequent analysis.

Conclusion:

Through SeriesClustera total of 26 subtype trend models were obtained，in which the19th,21st,22thsubtype trend models had significant upward trends from the control group to the TallCell Group,1,2,3,5,6,11subtype trend model has a significant downward trend from the Control sibgroup to the TallCell subgroup.

Screening differential subbtype trend expression gene

For the trend analysis results obtained above, the genes with high or low specificity at the subtype (time point) were screened and analyzed for intersection with the differentially expressed genes.

The Classical subtype :in the trend number 7th, 8th, 17th, 18th (132 genes)。

The Follicular subtype :in the trend number 2nd, 23rd, 11th, 14th (731 genes)。

TallCell subtype：in the trend number 0、12th、13th、25th（314 genes）。

The above subtype trend gene and the subtype differentially expressed genes were taken together to obtain a differential subtype trend expression gene, which is a differential gene in different subtypes of thyroid papillary carcinoma.

Conclusion:

Through the intersection analysisa total of 6 differential subtype trend expression genes (AQP6,HGFAC,HMGN2P19,MAGEE2,MST1L,PRSS50)were obtained in the classical subtype of papillary thyroid carcinoma；

Follicularhas 201 differential subtype trend-expressing genes；

TallCellhas 33 differential subtype trend-expressing genes；
