Papillary Thyroid Carcinoma related diagnostic biomarker target development and targeted drug selection

Author: Qianhui Yang        Research use only

  • 1.Intro(Introduction)
  • 2.Query(Genetic expression and clinical data from TCGA)
  • 3.DifGene(Screening differentially expressed genes of each subtype)
  • 4.seriescluster(Analyzing the differentially expressed genes of each subtype distinguished from other subtypes)
  • 5.Boxplot(DEG box plot)
  • 6.GOPathway(DEG KEGG and GO analysis)
  • 7.Mutation(analysis of the correlation between specific genes of each subtype and typical mutant gene)
  • 8.expressinmutdrivergene(analysis od the correlation between specific genes of each subtype and typical mutant gene)
  • 9.Stargene(specific gene and immune correlation analysis of each subtype)
  • 10.ImmuneScore(analysis of the correlation between specific genes and immune index and immune cell abundance of each subtype)
  • 11.GenePanel(screening survival-related gene panels in the classical subtype)
  • 12.GenePanel(screening the gene panel associated with lymph node involvement in each subtype)
  • 13.GeneSelect(the status of each subtype screening gene)
  • 14.Cmap(analysis of the correlation between genes associated with lymph node involvement in various subtype)
  • 15.Tool(software and database)
