Immune Score

CIBERSORT(https://cibersort.stanford.edu/)is a method to describe cell composition from gene expression profiles of complex tissue.

ESTIMATE(http://bioinformatics.mdanderson.org/estimate/rpackage.html)is an analytical tool for predicting tumor purity and can be used to predict the presence infiltrating stromal/immune cells in tumor tissue based on single-sample gene set enrichment analysis.

Method:

The relative abundance of 22 immune cells in each sample was calculated from CIBERSORT based on the expression profile data of the samples, and the corresponding immune index was calculated using ESTIMATE.

Correlation analysis between specific gene expression and immune cell abundance and immune index of each subtype was performed. Among them, the Follicular subtype selects genes in the kegg significant enrichment pathway.ã€‚

Conclusion:

By immunological index and immune abundance analysis,Follicular subtypeof thyroid papillary carcinoma has the lowest immune index,while the other two subtypes of Classical and TallCell subtypes have higher immune score

Specifically, specific genes in the Follicular subtypeare positively correlated withImmune_score,Macrophages M1,T cells gammadelta,T cells CD 4 memory activate and B cell naive.
