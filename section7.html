Gene Mutation Analysis

Methods:

THCA mutation data and the coresponding samples were downloaded from TCGA, and the R-package MAFtools is used to explore the potential link between the mutation and cancer。


Overall gene mutation analysis and statistics

Conclusion：

Among all the mutated genes of papillary thyroid carcinoma，BRAF gene has the highest mutation frequency (59%), followed by NRAS, TTN, TG, HRAS, MUC16, BDP1, HMCN1, KMT2A, MACF1, SPTA1 and EIF1AX

Among all mutation types，the missense mutation has the highest frequency, followed by frame shift mutation and nonsense mutation (nonsense mutation, stop codon mutation)；

The median number of mutations in mutant samples of papillary thyroid carcinoma is9 times.

Differential analysis of each subtype

In order to better study the phenotypic-related mutational gene variation, we need to understand the differential genetics between different groups and combine them with the phenotype.

In the data analysis process, fisher's exact test algorithm is used to calculate the degree of gene enrichment. A p-value < 0.05 differential mutant gene was selected as a unique mutant gene for each subtype.

Conclusion：

A total od 5 mutant differential genes are obtained by analysis；

Mutation differential gene of the Follicular subtype is NRAS, EIF1AX, HRAS;

The mutation differential gene of the Classical subtype is BRAF；

The mutation differential gene of the Tall Cell subtype is BRAF。
