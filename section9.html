Star Gene

The genes we are concerned with may not have been widely concerned and studied, and the mechanism of action is still unclear.

To further guide the research, we collected the proliferation, migration, and immune-related star genes in cancer, and classified them according to different functions and periods of action.

Correlation analysis is performed on the genes of our concern and the star genes of different classifications to further speculate on the potential function of the differential genes.

Methods:

We have collected star gene that has been extensively studied in cancer and classify them into apoptosis，EMT（epithelial interstitial transformation），proliferation，G0_G1（chromatin,protein replication）和G2_M（cell-cycle dividing）和 Immune microenviroment（cellular immune microenvrionment）。

For each type, we calculated the correlation between the unique gene and the star gene of each subtype based on the expression profile data and performed statistical tests. A scatter plot was drawn for each group of statistically significant genes, and all correlations under a certain classification were shown in a heat map.。

Conclusion:

Correlation analysis with star genes shows that among the three subtypes of thyroid papillary carcinoma，the specific genes of Follicular subtypeare closely related to cellular proliferation,EMT metastasis, and apoptosiswhile the unique genes ofTallCell subtypeis closely related to cell death and EMT transfer.
