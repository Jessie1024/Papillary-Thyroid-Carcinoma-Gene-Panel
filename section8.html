Gene Mutation Correlation Analysis

Method:

For the mutation analysis above, five genes(BRAF、NRAS、TTN、TG、HRAS) with highest mutation frequency are selected ，the box plot of the expression of the specific genes of each subtype gene was drawn according to whether the mutation was made.

Conclusion:In the classical subtype, AQP6，MAGEE2，BRAF，MST1L,TG mutation are significant;

In the follicular subtype, MAGEE2,MST1,LBRAF,CD1C,CCR4,BIRC3,BCL2A1,C1S,CFH,GRM7,GNAS,CNTN1 mutation are significant

In the TallCell subtype,MAGEE2,TG,MST1L,BRAF,MTCO2P2 mutation are significant.
