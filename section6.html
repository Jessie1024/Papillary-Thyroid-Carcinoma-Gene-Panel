GO analysis of DSTEGs

Method:

The differential genes obtained by the analysis are based on the GO annotations of the GO database from the three levels of BP(biological process), MF(molecular function), and CC(cellular component), and all the GOs in which the genes participate are obtained. The significance level (P-Value) of each GO was calculated by Fisher's test to screen out significant GOs with differential genes and negative correlation genes. Among them, the significant P-Value <0.01 is indicated by a yellow table.

Conclusion:

THCA Follicular DSTEGs are enriched in complement activation, regulation of immune response, complement activation of classical pathways etc.；

THCA TallCell DSTEGs are enriched in muscle organ development, extracellular matrix organization, negative regulation of metanepheric glomerular mesengial cell proliferation etc.；

THCA ClassicalDSTEGs are enriched in proteolysis, nitrate transport, glycerol transport etc.
